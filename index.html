About Us

Cek.eЯ is a web-based service that provides the easiest way to track shipments quickly and accurately. All information data regarding the status of your package delivery is accurately retrieved from each expedition website.

The advantage offered by Cek.eЯ is providing fast tracking status. Simply input your tracking number, and you will immediately know the status of your package being shipped.

Cek.eЯ is the result of a software engineering capstone project. It was developed by participants of the MSIB batch 5 who were undertaking independent studies in the Revou batch of August 2023. The Jayapura J19 group consists of 5 students from different universities in Indonesia.
